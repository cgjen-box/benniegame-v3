# Project Milestones: Bennie Bear Learning Game

---

## v1.0 MVP (Shipped: 2026-01-13)

**Delivered:** Complete autism-friendly iPad educational game with 4 activities, coin rewards, YouTube time exchange, and full parent controls.

**Phases completed:** 1-8 (23 plans total)

**Key accomplishments:**

1. **Design System** - 22 colors + typography matching autism-friendly constraints (96pt touch targets, no red/neon, positive-only feedback)

2. **Four Playable Activities** - Puzzle Matching, Labyrinth, Würfel (Dice), Wähle die Zahl (Numbers) — all with German UI

3. **Coin Economy** - Animated rewards, 5-coin celebration milestones with confetti

4. **YouTube Reward Loop** - TreasureView redemption (10 coins = 5 min, 20 coins = 12 min), VideoSelection, timed playback

5. **Audio System** - 3-channel AudioManager, ElevenLabs German voices, narrator and Bennie voice triggers

6. **Parent Controls** - Math gate, per-player dashboard, video management, daily time limits

7. **Asset Production** - 4-layer parallax backgrounds, 13 Lottie character animations via CDP automation

**Stats:**

- 95 files created/modified
- 8,230 lines of Swift
- 8 phases, 23 plans
- 2 days (2026-01-12 → 2026-01-13)

**Git range:** `feat(01-01)` → `feat(08-03)`

**What's next:** TestFlight deployment, real-world testing with Alexander & Oliver
