## Design Checklist

Before approving any image, verify:

- [ ] Colors are muted (saturation <80%)
- [ ] No harsh lighting/shadows
- [ ] Expression is warm and friendly
- [ ] Shapes are rounded, not angular
- [ ] Background is simple and calm
- [ ] Single clear focal point
- [ ] Feels safe and inviting
- [ ] Matches reference exactly
